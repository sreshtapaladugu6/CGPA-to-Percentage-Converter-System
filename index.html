About CGPA Conversion

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a grading system used by educational institutions to evaluate overall academic performance.

Conversion Formula

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

CGPA to Percentage Scale

10.0 CGPA= 95.0%
9.5 CGPA= 90.25%
9.0 CGPA= 85.5%

How to Calculate CGPA?

  1. Multiply each subject's grade point by its credit hours
  2. Add all the results
  3. Divide by the total number of credit hours

⚠️ Important Note

Different institutions may use different conversion formulas.
